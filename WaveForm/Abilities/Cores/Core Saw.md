#Red_friction 

# Normal Actions

Wally throws a physical saw forward, the saw pierces enemies 
depending on the orientation inputed the saw behaves differently

Up : After reaching the "fall point" the saw goes upward and does an arch to reach the player

Neutral : The saw goes forward, slows down and goes back to the player

Down : After reaching the "fall point" the saw goes down, in an arching motion to reach the player, if the saw hits the ground it rolls back to the player.

After using the core Wally enters a cooldown and can't use it.
If the saw hits the player the cooldown is reset.

The saws break when they hit a wall.

Goes Throught enemies

damage : 1
Knockback : 1
