#Purple_Gravity
 
# Normal Actions


With no movement input the player dashes backwards and create a black hole where he used to be, adding a move input allows to direct the dash (left / right)

The black hole attracts physical projectiles and deviates energy projectiles.

Damage : 2
Knockback : 2
___

# Context Action

Context Action : Spawns the black hole in a contained area, the player gravitates around it until the Core button is released.
It then propels the player in the direction.

___

# Passive

Wally becomes heavier, and becomes resistant to self KB
